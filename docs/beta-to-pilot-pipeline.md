# Beta To Pilot Pipeline

This document describes the public-safe sequence from iOS beta interest to paid pilot conversion.

## Sequence

1. Publish the LinkedIn beta invitation.
2. Route interested users to the iOS beta signup form.
3. Capture source, role, and consent in a Workspace-friendly record.
4. Deliver the 14-day freemium beta.
5. Capture retention, testimonial, and review signals.
6. Move the highest-signal users into pilot conversations.
7. Export the funnel evidence for the XPRIZE packet.

## Why this sequence matters

The contest cares about real users, real revenue, and working evidence.

This pipeline ensures consumer adoption contributes to:

- measurable beta signups
- testable retention
- public-safe social proof
- agency pilot leads
- revenue evidence

## Fields To Capture

- name
- email
- role
- organization type
- source channel
- beta consent
- pilot interest
- notes

## Conversion Rule

Beta interest is not revenue by itself.

Every beta lead should be scored for:

- engagement
- testimonial quality
- pilot fit
- privacy risk
- support burden

The top-scoring leads should be contacted for pilot conversion after the beta window.
