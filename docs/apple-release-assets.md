## Full Description Draft

Guardian Mosaic is a wellness-only iOS app that helps public-safety and responder communities build daily coherence habits.

It includes:

- daily check-ins
- short breathing and recovery prompts
- flow-state support
- sleep and recovery reflection
- aggregate pilot and sponsor reporting
- beta feedback capture

It does not provide diagnosis, treatment, medication advice, crisis response, or fitness-for-duty guidance.
