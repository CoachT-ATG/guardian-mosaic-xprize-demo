# iOS Beta Launch Checklist

This checklist turns the iOS freemium plan into a concrete launch sequence.

## What Must Exist Before Launch

- Apple ID with access to App Store Connect
- Apple Developer Program membership
- TestFlight access
- Google account access for Drive, Docs, Sheets, and Forms
- A Workspace sheet for beta leads and pilot leads
- A Workspace form for beta signup and feedback
- The public demo app package
- The beta-to-pilot funnel export

## What I Can Prepare Locally

- beta signup form
- pilot lead form
- funnel export
- Workspace-style sample data
- contest-safe launch copy
- evidence receipts

## What Requires Your Manual Authorization

- Apple Developer enrollment
- App Store Connect app creation
- TestFlight beta distribution setup
- any paid software purchase
- any external publication or posting

## Sequence

1. Prepare the iOS app build.
2. Prepare the beta signup intake.
3. Prepare Workspace tracking.
4. Publish the LinkedIn beta invitation.
5. Route signups to beta.
6. Convert the highest-signal beta users into pilot conversations.
7. Export evidence for XPRIZE.

## Deliverables To Collect

- beta signup count
- activation count
- day-2 and day-7 retention
- testimonials
- pilot leads
- pilot closes
- revenue receipts
