# Above The Genes E-Commerce Plan

## Objective

Turn `abovethegenes.com` into the revenue engine for Above The Genes while Guardian Mosaic progresses from XPRIZE-facing responder wellness app to the broader Organizational Singularity platform.

## Current Site Read

The public site already has:

- executive performance and flow positioning
- founder credibility from law enforcement and intelligence experience
- coaching call-to-action
- blog and education content
- cart/e-commerce surface
- wellness, genetics, epigenetics, biometrics, and resilience language

The immediate gap is conversion architecture: clear offers, products, pricing, checkout, lead capture, evidence, testimonials, and a funnel that routes visitors into beta, coaching, pilots, and later platform subscriptions.

## Strategic Positioning

Public positioning:

`Above The Genes helps high-stress professionals and teams improve resilience, recovery, purpose, and performance through AI-assisted wellness, coaching, and evidence-based behavior change.`

Private canon:

The full Organizational Singularity, Mosaic Organism, Hermes, `/goal`, corpus intelligence, and advanced sensorium remain the private operating architecture. Public pages should expose benefits, evidence, and safe wellness language, not patent-sensitive internals.

## First Products To Build

### Product 1: Free Guardian Mosaic Beta

- Price: free
- Purpose: users, testimonials, XPRIZE evidence
- CTA: `Join the Guardian Mosaic beta`
- Data captured: name, email, role, organization, consent, referral source

### Product 2: Responder Recovery Reset

- Price: `$19-$49`
- Format: downloadable guide + email sequence
- Purpose: first revenue, non-clinical, easy checkout
- Promise: practical recovery habits for sleep, stress, decompression, and purpose

### Product 3: Performance Zone Strategy Session

- Price: `$149-$299`
- Format: one-time coaching/session booking
- Purpose: founder-led revenue and customer discovery
- Boundary: wellness education and coaching, not medical advice

### Product 4: Agency Pilot Discovery

- Price: free application, then paid pilot proposal
- Format: intake form + call
- Purpose: XPRIZE business viability and revenue path
- Target: law enforcement, fire/EMS, dispatch, veterans groups, academies

### Product 5: Guardian Mosaic Pilot

- Price: small paid pilot, then recurring agency subscription
- Format: app access + aggregate reporting + implementation support
- Purpose: core revenue model
