## Chosen Category

Guardian Mosaic is governed under:

`Education & Human Potential`

Category framing: transforming how people learn, grow, and achieve their best. Guardian Mosaic maps to this through responder wellness, recovery, resilience, purpose, and daily human-performance support.

## Hard Eligibility / Submission Gates

The public submission must:

- be a business that operates with AI
- demonstrate or explain how AI transforms business workflows
- use at least one Google Cloud product
- use Gemini API for at least one LLM call in the deployed application if LLM functionality is included
- disclose how any pre-existing code, templates, frameworks, or assets were used
- include a code repository containing necessary source code
- be public with relevant licensing or private and shared with `testing@devpost.com` and `judging@hacker.fund`
- include a demo video under 3 minutes
- show the project functioning on its intended device/platform
- avoid third-party trademarks, copyrighted music, or copyrighted material without permission
- provide product-running evidence such as logs, API usage records, dashboard screenshots, or continuous playbook receipts
- provide revenue, user, cost, marketing spend, and related-party revenue evidence, even when values are zero
- provide free judging/testing access through the judging period
- keep all submission materials in English

## Business Evidence Gates

Before final submission, the repo/submission must contain:

- total revenue during the hackathon period
- revenue by month for May, June, July, and August 2026
- related-party revenue reported separately
- total costs
- cost of goods sold
- marketing and customer-acquisition spend
- additional expenses
- number of users acquired
- number of paying users
- public testimonial or clear statement if none exists yet
- financial evidence file, even if revenue is zero

## Product Evidence Gates

Before final submission, the repo/submission must contain:

- working app or demo access
- GitHub repository link
- product-running evidence link
- Gemini/Google evidence link
- demo video link
- screenshots/media under platform limits
- testing instructions
- no private PHI, secrets, or proprietary internals in public artifacts

## Judging Criteria Alignment

All promoted work must improve at least one criterion:

- Business Viability: real business, real users, revenue path, sustainable model.
- AI-Native Operations: AI live in production or demo path, executing bounded workflow decisions.
- Category Impact: clear Education & Human Potential impact for public-safety wellness and human flourishing.

## Guardian Mosaic Governance Rules

1. Public artifacts stay wellness-only.
2. No diagnosis, treatment, medication advice, crisis automation, or fitness-for-duty decisions.
3. Proprietary Mosaic Organism, 6D tesseract, corpus internals, and patent-sensitive logic stay private unless explicitly sanitized.
4. AI recommendations are conservative wellness suggestions, not clinical determinations.
5. Sensorium inputs are low-confidence trends.
6. Every claim in the submission must have an evidence path or be framed as roadmap/future work.
7. The repository must remain judge-runnable and free of secrets.
8. Demo video must be less than 3 minutes and show the actual app/demo functioning.
9. Google/Gemini use must be visible in code, docs, and evidence receipts.
10. No final promotion if a required evidence gate is stale, missing, yellow, or red.
