# Guardian Mosaic Agency Pilot Offer

## Offer

Guardian Mosaic is a 30-day wellness-only pilot for public-safety teams, first responders, veterans, and high-stress operational groups.

The pilot helps participants build daily habits around recovery, stress reset, sleep reflection, focus, purpose, and peer-support awareness.

## Who It Is For

- law enforcement agencies
- fire / EMS teams
- dispatch centers
- training academies
- peer-support teams
- veteran and military-adjacent organizations
- municipal wellness leaders

## What Participants Receive

- daily check-ins
- conservative wellness prompts
- breathwork and recovery suggestions
- sleep and stress reflection
- purpose and flow prompts
- beta feedback capture

## What Sponsors Receive

- aggregate engagement summary
- completed check-in count
- retention trend
- qualitative feedback summary
- pilot recommendations
- public-safe evidence packet if the sponsor approves

## Safety Boundary

Guardian Mosaic does not diagnose, treat, provide medication guidance, make crisis-response decisions, or make fitness-for-duty or return-to-duty determinations.

All recommendations are wellness education and conservative habit support.

## Starter Pilot Structure

- Duration: 30 days
- Seats: 10-25 participants
- Format: iOS beta plus lightweight reporting
- Setup: sponsor intake, beta access, weekly review, final summary
- Success metric: daily engagement, retention, participant feedback, and sponsor willingness to continue
