# Corpus Priority

This public demo uses a deliberately narrow corpus slice from the broader Flow research library.

## Priority Lanes

1. Public-safety resilience
1. Mindfulness and stress regulation
1. Flow and performance under challenge
1. Sleep, recovery, and HRV
1. Breathwork and self-regulation
1. Exercise, training, and tactical performance

## Keep In The Public Demo

- first responder resilience and wellness
- law enforcement stress and recovery
- HRV and psychophysiology
- sleep quality and cognitive performance
- flow state and attention control
- breathwork and mindfulness
- exercise and operational readiness

## Keep Out Of The Public Demo

- psychedelic therapy and retreat operations
- clinical treatment claims
- diagnosis or medication advice
- PHI or private patient records
- any content that would weaken the contest-safe public scope

## Use Pattern

- score filenames by relevance to the responder-coherence wedge
- route high-signal items into the local ingestion queue
- keep the public repo limited to the safe selection policy, not the private source path
- retain a local manifest for the full private corpus

## Result

The Flow corpus becomes a high-density, low-risk evidence base for the `ATG Responder Coherence Operator` demo without exposing the broader private research archive.
